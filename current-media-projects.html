Current Media Projects

Harmony

Here are the media projects I'm currently working on, and what they mean.

MARVEL COMICS: The last 20-ish years of Marvel Comics have changed the face of superhero media, and media in general. Also, I just generally love Marvel in general. The Marvel project, currently, is to read just about every issue of Marvel Comics starting around the turn of the millennium.

SHONEN JUMP: Midway thru 2020, I decided to catch up with all the currently running series in Weekly Shonen Jump. I'll post my thoughts on the series as I go thru them.

HISTORY OF VIDEO GAMES: This one's a bit harder to explain, but basically: I missed most video games growing up, and there's a lot I want to get to. I put together a timeline of video games that starts with every game represented in the Super Smash Bros. series (which includes a lot of the classics in some way), and then expanded with a few other collections and oeuvres, like recent Bethesda and Eidos Interactive games, plus Rare Replay, recent mini consoles, etc. Thru this project, I will play as many of these games as I can. Anything I can't find at all, I'll at least try to track down some footage to get an idea.
